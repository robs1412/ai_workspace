# Avignon CC Learning Ledger

Last Updated: 2026-04-26

## Purpose

When Sonat copies Avignon on an email thread, Avignon should treat the thread as a learning source for future recall of Sonat's preferences, answers, positioning, account logic, wording, and decision patterns.

## Operating Rule

- If Sonat only CCs Avignon and does not ask for action, do not send a captured/routed acknowledgement or completion note for that thread.
- Review the thread for reusable business lessons: how Sonat answered the question, what commitments she did or did not make, what wording she used, and what account/distributor/market principle should be remembered.
- Record only concise, non-secret lessons in this ledger or the relevant Avignon TODO/HANDOFF/decision state.
- Do not paste private mailbox bodies, credentials, tokens, private links, or sensitive customer/vendor details into this ledger.
- If the CC'd thread includes a clear instruction to Avignon, a routine internal task, an approval, or a blocker, handle it through the normal direct-owner or routed-worker workflow instead of treating it as passive learning.
- If the lesson affects CRM, Portal, OPS, pricing, distributor support, DA/funding, legal/compliance, or external promises, preserve the normal approval gates and do not infer commitments beyond Sonat's actual response.
