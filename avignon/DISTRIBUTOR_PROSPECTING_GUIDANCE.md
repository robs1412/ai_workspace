# Distributor Prospecting Guidance

Last Updated: 2026-04-30

Source: Sonat direct Avignon email, subject `Prospective distributor email`, received 2026-04-27. Keep the private source in mailbox state; this file is the durable working template for future Avignon drafts.

## When To Use

Use this when Sonat asks Avignon to draft an email to a prospective distributor.

The output should be a draft for Sonat to review unless Sonat separately approves an external send target and final text.

## Default Structure

1. Open directly to the named distributor/contact.
2. Say Sonat has followed the portfolio and sees a possible fit between the companies.
3. Include the KOVAL brand presentation link when appropriate:
   `https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1ieKgdIl7Khvvg0eoqROkhxT2qVheDNCZ/edit?usp=sharing&ouid=101551591411211579369&rtpof=true&sd=true`
4. Add the KOVAL company/founder credibility paragraph.
5. Use short bullets for why KOVAL fits the distributor's book or market.
6. Offer core and Thresh & Winnow sell sheets when relevant.
7. Ask to send a sampling kit to the distributor's office.

## Possible Fit Bullets

Adapt the bullets to the recipient's market and portfolio. Do not use every point if it makes the draft heavy.

- Regenerative and organic integrity: KOVAL sources grain from local organic farmers.
- On-premise profitability: Cranberry Gin Liqueur can be positioned as a premium organic alternative for cocktail programs.
- Thresh & Winnow: collector-tier, high-end back-bar language; 10-year Rye and brandy-cask finishes may fit allocated wine or premium spirits buyers.
- Verified velocity: use only verified current mandates, menu placements, airline/lounge facts, or account pull-through evidence. Re-check live facts before making a time-sensitive claim.

## Guardrails

- Avoid unsupported claims about current mandates, market velocity, or buyer demand unless verified for the draft date.
- Do not promise samples, pricing, DA support, exclusivity, allocation, delivery timing, or account commitments without Sonat approval.
- Keep the language polished and direct. Sonat supplied the template as the starting voice; improve clarity without turning it into technical AI prose.
