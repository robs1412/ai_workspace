## Market Workflow Direction

- Use a two-phase monthly rhythm once data access is settled:
  - Weeks 1, 3, and 4: leading indicators, CRM activity, rep visits, samples, follow-ups, contact cleanup, and account-level nudges.
  - Week 2: previous-month hard depletions, shipments, month-over-month velocity, market slippage, and root-cause review.
- Prepare a Friday review packet when there are enough active drafts:
  - High priority: time-sensitive RFPs, important relationship saves, and funding/DA negotiations.
  - Standard maintenance: distributor check-ins, 90-day inactive account nudges, and reengagement drafts.
  - Logistics: brand-refresh trackers, contact cleaning, and visual proof of placement updates.
- Prioritize market intelligence in this order unless Sonat redirects:
  - Illinois and local blitz/core-restoration work.
  - Best-performing high-proof markets and why they are winning.
  - Slipping or stagnant markets with root-cause analysis.
  - Competitive noise and field feedback.
- For inactive accounts, provide a nudge package rather than a raw alert.
- For whitespace/new POD opportunities, identify missing products in strong accounts and propose the next account/distributor action.
- For distributor support or funding requests, ask for projected case lift, specific account list, and ROI context before recommending investment.
