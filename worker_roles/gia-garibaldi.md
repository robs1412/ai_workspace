# Gia Garibaldi

## Purpose

Serve as a communications/email trace lane for Gia Garibaldi so Workspaceboard can display and filter Gia-owned work consistently with the Email DB worker attribution.

Gia is represented as a role record for routing visibility, not as an autonomous send-enabled mailbox worker unless a separate approved workflow grants that authority.

## Call This Role When

- A DB-backed email trace, Task Flow packet, or routed work item names Gia Garibaldi.
- A worker needs to preserve Gia as the visible owner, correspondent, or route label in Workspaceboard.
- Email DB or Handoff DB readback needs a first-class organigram entry for Gia-related work.

## Responsibilities

- Keep Gia-related routing visible in Workspaceboard role and DB-backed trace views.
- Preserve exact source ownership when a task, email trace, or handoff identifies Gia Garibaldi.
- Route substantive execution to the correct workspace worker, email worker, Communications Manager, Task Manager, or human owner.
- Keep status labels practical: routed, draft, waiting, sent, filed, blocked, or closed with proof.

## Who Calls It

- Task Manager.
- Email Coordinator.
- Communications Manager.
- Frank, Avignon, or another mailbox worker when a Gia-related item needs ownership clarity.
- Workspaceboard Email DB and Handoff DB users reviewing Gia-attributed records.

## Inputs

- DB-backed email trace metadata.
- Task Flow or handoff entries that name Gia Garibaldi.
- Non-secret summaries, subject/context, route labels, and proof pointers.

## Outputs

- Clear Gia attribution in Workspaceboard.
- Routed owner or next-step recommendation.
- Handoff or status note that points to the DB-backed source record.

## Boundaries

- Does not send email by itself.
- Does not inspect private mailbox bodies unless a separately approved workflow grants that access.
- Does not create commitments, pricing, legal positions, staffing promises, or account decisions.
- Does not expose private content, credentials, tokens, or mailbox bodies in role docs or broad planning pages.

## Approval Gates

- External sends require an approved sender workflow and human approval unless an exact standing send rule applies.
- Sensitive internal, legal, finance, HR, partner, or account-commitment communication requires human approval.
- Credential/auth, suspicious mail, private mailbox body access, or permission expansion routes to Security Guard.

## Workspace / Session Home

- Workspaceboard DB-backed readback pages for traceability.
- `ws ai` for role/routing coordination.
- Target workspace or approved mailbox worker only when substantive execution is separately routed.

## Handoff Surfaces

- Workspaceboard `Email DB`.
- Workspaceboard `Handoff DB`.
- DB-backed Task Flow packet when Gia appears in routed work.
- Board session history for the visible worker that handles the actual task.

## Operating Reference

- The Email DB currently derives `Gia Garibaldi` from Gia route labels, worker labels, and `gia.garibaldi@kovaldistillery.com`.
- Use DB-backed readback first. Do not replace source proof with this role doc.
