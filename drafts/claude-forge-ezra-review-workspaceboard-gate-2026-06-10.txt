Please update the Forge social-posting approval process so Ezra review is a real Workspaceboard / Task Flow approval gate, not just an email notice.

Recommended implementation:

1. Detect when a Forge Social Posting planner item or post draft needs Ezra review.
Trigger this for alcohol-related posts, including KOVAL product, alcohol consumption, tasting/event promotion, retailer/distributor references, product claims, paid/boosted media, influencer content, "where to buy" language, or state-specific alcohol marketing/distribution context.

2. Consider the Ezra guide before the post is created, or at least before the first approval draft is treated as ready.
The post creator should consult the overarching themes in the guide early: TTB ad basics, no misleading product/health/effect claims, label/COLA consistency, age/youth-appeal guardrails, responsible-use framing, retailer/distributor/tied-house caution, and state-specific review for event/retailer/distribution content.

Guide:
https://docs.google.com/document/d/1QEDGQARePX8kL6R88K6UN9fdl4K7KQf-b0urjUjumxE/edit?usp=drivesdk

3. Create or reuse a visible Workspaceboard / Task Flow review packet for Ezra/Codex.
The packet should include:
- Forge planner row/post id and title
- linked OPS task, especially social posting OPS task 369890 when applicable
- draft caption/copy
- image/video/link references
- target platform
- proposed post date
- product/SKU/COLA if known
- retailer, distributor, event, tasting, or where-to-buy references
- paid/boosted/influencer flag
- target state/geography
- the Ezra guide link above

4. Store the review state on the Forge approval record.
Suggested fields:
- ezra_review_status: not_needed, pending, clear, clear_with_edits, blocked
- ezra_review_taskflow_key
- ezra_review_workspaceboard_session
- ezra_review_proof
- ezra_reviewed_at
- ezra_review_note

5. Block final posting approval while Ezra review is pending or blocked.
Final post approval should require ezra_review_status to be clear, clear_with_edits, or not_needed. If clear_with_edits, require the final copy to show the edits applied or a recorded Robert/Sonat/Mark/Dmytro/Sebastian/counsel override.

6. Allow manual override only with audit.
Robert, Sonat, Mark, Dmytro, Sebastian, or counsel can override pending/blocked Ezra review, but Forge should store who overrode it, when, and why.

The important part: Forge should not treat "consult Ezra" as a passive checkbox. The controlling proof should be the Forge approval record plus Workspaceboard / Task Flow review proof.
