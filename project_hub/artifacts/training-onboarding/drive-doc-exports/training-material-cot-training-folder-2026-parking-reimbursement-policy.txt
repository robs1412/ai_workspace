Parking Reimbursement Policy
Effective Date: 10/24/2025
Purpose
This policy provides guidance on parking reimbursements for team members attending work-related events, such as tastings. The goal is to ensure reasonable and cost-effective use of company funds.
Policy
1. Eligibility for Reimbursement
   * Team members may be reimbursed for parking expenses incurred while attending approved work-related activities.
   * Reimbursement will only be made for reasonable and necessary parking costs.
2. Cost Expectations
   * Employees are expected to select the most cost-effective parking option available.
   * Prior to travel, team members should use tools such as SpotHero, ParkWhiz, or similar services to compare parking rates and plan ahead.
   * The company will not reimburse for unnecessarily expensive parking choices when lower-cost options were reasonably available.
3. Documentation Requirements
   * Reimbursement requests must include:
      * A receipt or digital confirmation showing the parking charge.
      * Location and purpose of the visit or event in the reimbursement notes
4. Reasonableness Standard
   * While parking rates vary by location, any parking charge that appears significantly higher than typical area rates may be subject to review or adjustment prior to reimbursement.
   * As a general guideline, parking expenses exceeding $25 for short events (under 4 hours) or $40 for full-day events should include a brief justification.


5. Manager Responsibility
   * Managers should ensure their team members are aware of and adhere to this policy.
   * Questions or exceptions should be discussed with the Management team in advance when possible.
