Tips & Tricks













Time Track
My time
* Click on a day with a checkmark to see a breakdown of hours worked
* To add time
   * Hit the small ‘+’ button
   * Under ‘Day Type’, select ‘Forgotten Day’
   * Select the date
   * Select the time
      * Please submit in military time
   * Under ‘Notes’, please list why you are submitting a forgotten time
      * i.e: I forgot to clock in for my Whole Foods - Halsted tasting from 12:00-3:00
   * Hit ‘Save’
* Time Track How to Video
Important Dates
* A list of company holidays














Payroll
My Payroll
* Allows you to see weekly pay, reimbursements, pay additions, bonus payments, and gross payment
   * Select timeframe you want to view
* This information can also be found in OnPay
Payroll Calendar
* Allows you to see beginning and end of pay period, the payroll run date (the day you will receive an email from OnPay), and the pay date






















Reimbursements
My Reimbursements
* Suburban Tasting
   * Enter .5 for tastings over 12 miles
   * Enter .75 for tastings over 20 miles
   * List the miles and destination under ‘Notes’
   * Mileage Reimbursement Policy
* Tolls & Parking
   * Add receipt under ‘Related Documents’
* Mileage
   * Mileage Reimbursement Policy
   * List the miles and destinations under ‘Notes’
* Expenses
   * Purchases like mixers
      * Spirits should not be purchased at an account unless discussed with a manager beforehand
   * List the expenses under ‘Notes’
   * Add receipt under ‘Related Documents’
* Save and view to ensure everything is correct
* Reimbursements should be submitted as soon as possible to ensure you are reimbursed in a timely manner












Account Touchpoints
My Activities
* View all of your submitted activities
   * View based on timeframe
* Add new activity
   * Select ‘+ Create’
   * Select Category
   * Select Account
   * Enter Activity Date
   * Enter time of activity
      * In military time
   * Under ‘Description’, answer tasting questions
      * 1. What SKUs are they carrying?
      * 2. Is anything low or out of stock?
      * 3. What did you sample, and how many of each?
      * 4. How many bottles of what were sold?
      * 5. If not listed in the notes, what is the buyer's name?
      * 6. What was the foot traffic like?
         * If the buyer/manager/associate mentions their best time and days for foot traffic, please include that as well.
      * 7. Note any problems the buyer/manager/associate mentions (i.e. bad shipment, an ordered SKU not arriving, etc.)
         * Please also send me a direct message
      * 8. Note any interesting conversations, contact information you received from potential accounts, event requests, etc.
   * Fill out section under ‘Tastings’

POS and Samples
Sample Request
1. Hit the ‘+ Create’ button on the left side of the screen
2. Under Account, select ‘KOVAL’
3. Under Date Needed, select your date
   1. Please try to request samples with at least a week's notice
4. Type in Notes how many of each sample you need OR
5. Under Request Items, select the bottle icon to how many of each product you need
   1. Thresh & Winnow is not currently on the list, so please use the Notes section to list any Thresh & Winnow samples you need.
   2. For T&W samples, please list them as minis (i.e. T&W Citrine and Foret minis)
6. Under Shipping Details, list your first and last name, phone number, and select ‘Pick Up’ under Delivery Type
7. Shipping samples is on a case-by-case basis, so please contact Macee if this applies to you.
8. Hit ‘Save and View’ to ensure the request submitted correctly
