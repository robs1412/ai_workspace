2025 Koval Manual
(last update: September 10, 2025)


Welcome to the Koval Manual – a resource designed to support your day-to-day work and supplement our formal Employee Handbook. While the Handbook covers our core policies and legal commitments, this guide focuses on practical topics: how we communicate, where to find things, how to manage information, and what’s expected in our shared work environment.


Whether you're new to Koval or a long-time team member, this guide is here to help you work efficiently, stay secure, and represent the company well – internally and externally.


How to Use This Manual Efficiently


To make this guide easy to navigate, we've structured it around short summaries for each key topic, with embedded links to more detailed documents, templates, or internal pages.


Tips for Using This Manual:


* Scan the Table of Contents to jump straight to the topic you need
* Read the section summary for a quick overview
* Click the embedded link (highlighted in blue) to access the full document or resource
* Use Ctrl+F (Windows) or Cmd+F (Mac) to quickly find specific words or topics
* Bookmark this guide for quick access when questions come up
* Some links open Google Docs, Shared Drive folders, or internal templates – make sure you're logged into your Koval account


This guide works best when used as a living document. Check back occasionally, as sections may be updated with the latest tools or best practices.
