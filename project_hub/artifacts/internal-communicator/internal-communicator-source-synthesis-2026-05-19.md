## Role Interpretation

- `Internal Communicator` should own the staff-facing update layer, especially recurring internal updates and general company information.
- `Communications Manager` should still own draft quality and send-readiness, but not collapse into the internal news function.
- `Marketing Manager` should remain separate for external/campaign-style sends.
- The source does not suggest a new marketing persona.
- The source does suggest that internal communication deserves a more explicit cadence and source-packet structure.
