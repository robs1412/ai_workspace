AI Workers JDs Guide

Concise working guide for the active worker set. 2026 JDs are primary; 2023 JDs are legacy fallback only. Use the full FOH guide for operational detail and the worker JOB_DESCRIPTION.md files for ownership.

Purpose

  • This guide is the working map for the current worker set.
  • Customer Service remains blocked until the role is approved and the inbox connection is opened.
  • Use the local job descriptions as the checked-off source unless a canonical Google Doc link is explicitly recorded.

Role Families

RoleWhat it owns
Frank
  • Robert-facing chief-of-staff mailbox worker.
  • Owns capture, routing, follow-through, completion reporting, and direct-owner follow-up for Robert-directed work.
  • The Frank lane is for direct-owner work, not generic inbox summaries.
  • Source: `frank/JOB_DESCRIPTION.md`; `worker_roles/frank-cannoli.md`.
Avignon
  • Sonat-facing chief-of-staff mailbox worker.
  • Owns capture, routing, follow-through, CRM/distributor/market-execution follow-through, and completion reporting.
  • The Avignon lane is for Sonat-directed work and direct-owner follow-up.
  • Source: `avignon/JOB_DESCRIPTION.md`; `worker_roles/avignon-rose.md`.
Vanessa Sterling / Outreach Coordinator
  • The outreach role is Vanessa Sterling; National Outreach is the mailbox, not the persona.
  • Owns outreach coordination, tasting scheduling, OPS updates, and weekly follow-through.
  • The National Outreach mailbox is intake-only.
  • Source: `nationaloutreach/JOB_DESCRIPTION.md`; `worker_roles/outreach-coordinator.md`; `project_hub/artifacts/ai-workers-setup/vanessa-tasting-directive-index-2026-05-17.md`.
Communications Manager
  • Owns outbound copy, tone, and send-readiness across customer, distributor, partner, media, and larger staff communication.
  • Coordinates drafts, revisions, and approval flow, but does not own send mechanics or campaign operations.
  • Source: `worker_roles/communications-manager.md`.
Email Coordinator
  • Owns routing and ownership across Frank, Avignon, National Outreach, and OPS/Portal follow-up.
  • Enforces send-from/persona mapping and keeps email-derived work from disappearing in inboxes.
  • Source: `worker_roles/email-coordinator.md`.
Internal Communicator
  • Owns internal staff-facing notes, reminders, follow-ups, and human-readable internal status updates.
  • Keeps broad internal announcements approval-gated.
  • Source: `worker_roles/internal-communicator.md`; `project_hub/artifacts/internal-communicator/weekly-internal-recap-workflow-packet-2026-05-07.md`.
Marketing Manager
  • Owns organized marketing sends through Forge or another approved sender.
  • Handles distributor, magazine, media, and campaign-style send planning.
  • Coordinates with Communications Manager for copy and with Forge for mechanics.
  • Source: `worker_roles/marketing-manager.md`.
Naomi
  • Owns finance operations, cadence, controls, budget/reporting coordination, reminders, invoice follow-through, and finance intake routing.
  • Not send-enabled.
  • Built from `naomi/JOB_DESCRIPTION.md` plus `project_hub/artifacts/ai-workers-setup/naomi-finance-task-map-2026-05-17.md`.
Ezra
  • Owns project support and legal-affairs-adjacent triage.
  • Handles intake framing, escalation, routing, and docs-first support.
  • Not a final legal decision-maker.
  • Source: `ezra/JOB_DESCRIPTION.md`; `project_hub/artifacts/ai-workers-setup/ezra-project-support-intake-checklist-2026-05-17.md`.
Asher
  • Heritage, stewardship, provenance, food, farming, craft, and slow-travel storytelling.
  • Use for warm, literary, grounded storytelling and editorial outreach.
  • Source: `asher/JOB_DESCRIPTION.md`; `worker_roles/asher.md`; `docs/email-workers/2026-04-27-asher-venetia-setup.md`.
Venetia
  • Sustainability, material innovation, circular systems, future-facing design, and policy stories.
  • Use for sharper, design-forward, policy-aware editorial work.
  • Source: `venetia/JOB_DESCRIPTION.md`; `worker_roles/venetia.md`; `docs/email-workers/2026-04-27-asher-venetia-setup.md`.
AI Manager Robert
  • Top control-lane role for priorities, approvals, chain-of-command, and final business direction.
  • This is the human management surface, not a hidden execution lane.
  • Source: `ai-manager/JOB_DESCRIPTION.md`; `worker_roles/ai-manager-robert.md`.
Task Manager / Polier
  • Owns board routing, visibility, durable state, worker ownership, cleanup, and blocker surfacing.
  • Starts or focuses visible workers and keeps the board from hiding implementation.
  • Source: `task-manager/JOB_DESCRIPTION.md`; `worker_roles/task-manager-polier.md`.
Decision Driver
  • Pushes waiting work into one safe next action or one concrete human decision.
  • Does not become a parking state.
  • Source: `decision-driver/JOB_DESCRIPTION.md`; `worker_roles/decision-driver.md`.
AI Health Manager
  • Monitors board/session health and stale workers.
  • Nudges safe stale workers once.
  • Does not implement business work or alter runtimes.
  • Source: `ai-health-manager/JOB_DESCRIPTION.md`; `worker_roles/ai-health-manager.md`.
Customer Service / Guest Experience
  • Working persona name: Guest Experience.
  • Blocked until the role is explicitly approved and the inbox is connected.
  • Use the FOH guide for FAQ and answer authority.
  • Source: `project_hub/artifacts/ai-workers-setup/customer-service-inbox-faq-setup-brief-2026-05-17.md`; `project_hub/artifacts/ai-workers-setup/foh-handbook-2-guide.md`.

Persona and Role Boundaries

  • Communications Manager, Email Coordinator, Internal Communicator, Marketing Manager, and Outreach Coordinator are already separate roles; do not add new personas for them unless a future split creates a genuinely different voice or approval boundary.
  • If the 2026 versus 2023 comparison reveals a real change in speaking authority, add a persona only for that lane.
  • Customer Service is the main future persona candidate; Guest Experience is the current working name.
  • Naomi does not need a separate controller doc for the current guide; her current source is the local JD plus the finance task map.
