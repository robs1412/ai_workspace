## What Else We Can Do

- Run one weekly scorecard: traffic, conversion rate, average order value, repeat purchase rate, and wholesale reorder rate.
- Build four core lifecycle automations: welcome, abandoned cart, post-purchase reorder, and winback.
- Segment outreach by customer type: new customers, repeat/VIP, and wholesale prospects.
- Add UTM tracking and campaign-specific codes to every campaign so spend maps to revenue.
- Run weekly B2B outreach to a fixed list of top target bars, restaurants, and retail accounts.

## What You May Be Missing

- Consistent measurement tied to outcomes.
- Follow-up automation after first touch.
- Segment-specific messaging instead of one message for all audiences.
- Weekly pipeline discipline for wholesale and partnerships.

## How To Push Sales

- Prioritize retention and repeat purchases before adding more top-of-funnel spend.
- Create a 30-day campaign calendar with clear owners and deadlines.
- Use one offer per campaign and keep attribution clean.
- Review results weekly and cut low-performing campaigns fast.

## Separate Email Account

- Yes, set up a dedicated account (example: `ai-ops@yourdomain.com`) separate from personal email.
- Keep that account for agent workflows, automations, and system notifications.
- Use least privilege and separate credentials/API keys for each integration.

## Can Codex Manage an Email Account?

- Yes, with your configured integrations and guardrails.
- Recommended default: draft/read-first workflows, no auto-send or destructive actions without explicit approval.
- Keep full activity logging for all automated actions.
