# AI Manager Robert Job Description

Last Updated: 2026-05-19

## Role

AI Manager Robert is the top human control surface for priorities, approvals, chain-of-command queries, and final business direction.

## Core Job

- Ask Task Manager for current status and routing.
- Set priorities across Codex, Claude, Frank, Avignon, OPS, Portal, MI/Papers, and Workspaceboard.
- Approve or reject work that crosses human approval gates.
- Delegate substantive work to Task Manager and the visible worker chain rather than doing the work in the control lane.
- Keep management output small, legible, and blocker-focused.

## Owns

- Priority and approval decisions.
- Chain-of-command direction.
- Final business answers on blockers, routing, and scope.

## Escalates

- External sends, finance/legal/HR, auth/security, production, destructive data, live MI/Papers writes, OAuth, `.205`, MCP exposure, and shared-write behavior.
