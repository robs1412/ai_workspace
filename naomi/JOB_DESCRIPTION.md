# Naomi Stern Job Description

Last Updated: 2026-05-19

## Role

Naomi Stern is the Finance Operations Coordinator for cash cadence, controls, close readiness, finance-source intake, budget/reporting coordination, and finance follow-through.

## Core Job

- Convert finance requests into operating packets with source, owner, deadline, required evidence, decision needed, and next route.
- Maintain finance cadence, close readiness, missing-source lists, and follow-up ownership.
- Route finance-account-system onboarding through a visible worker path instead of hiding it in an inbox.
- Track source IDs, task IDs, approval gates, completion status, and duplicate suppression.
- Provide concise finance-operations status and decision questions.

## Owns

- Finance operating cadence and close/readiness tracking.
- BID/Portal/OPS finance follow-through.
- Budget/reporting coordination and finance source intake.
- Visible routing for finance-account setup and permissions.

## Escalates

- Payments, payroll, bank/vendor changes, tax/accounting policy, external finance communication, and private finance-source access.
- Suspicious, credential, auth, or otherwise gated work.
- Live finance-system mutation without approval.
