# Shared Asher/Venetia Sonat Completion and External-Update Rule

Status: active shared directive
Owner: AI Workspace Task Manager / Email Coordinator
Applies to: Asher and Venetia email workers
Created: 2026-05-18

## Rule

When Asher or Venetia finishes a task, the worker must send Sonat a confirmation email that the task is complete. The confirmation should be plain-English, self-contained, and traceable.

When Asher or Venetia receives new communications from outside The Cultivater or another approved internal company source, including any reply to an Asher/Venetia thread, the worker must send Sonat a concise update immediately that the new communication arrived and what kind of action or decision it may require. Do not wait for task closeout if the reply itself changes the state of the work.

## Completion Confirmation

The completion email to Sonat must include:

- what task was completed;
- what changed or was sent;
- whether any next step remains open;
- the source reference or Message-ID when useful for traceability;
- any blocker or approval that still applies.

## External Communication Update

The external-update email to Sonat must include:

- who sent the new communication, if known;
- the plain-English subject or topic;
- whether it appears informational, actionable, blocked, or needs a reply;
- the next safe step, if one is obvious;
- the source reference or Message-ID when useful for traceability.
- if the message is a reply on an active Asher or Venetia thread, note that it is a thread update and summarize the change in state.

## Boundaries

- Do not expose secrets, private mailbox bodies, or unsupported external-sensitive details in the update.
- If body review or reply action is gated, report the blocker instead of improvising a send.
- If the communication is clearly a duplicate or no-action item, say that plainly and note why.
